Advanced Polymer Science Meets Energy Excellence

About Rosehill Energy

With over two decades of pioneering innovation, we specialise in formulating and manufacturing bespoke polymer systems that excel in the toughest marine and onshore environments.

Our Story

Where advanced polymer science meets the rigorous demands of the energy sector

At Rosehill Energy, we have built our reputation on pushing the boundaries of polymer chemistry to deliver solutions that outperform traditional materials in the most demanding environments.

Our state-of-the-art continuous reactor technology not only facilitates rapid innovation but also ensures faster production, significantly reducing lead times. This capability allows us to quickly deliver polymer solutions that excel in hot-wet conditions, provide superior adhesion, and offer exceptional abrasion resistance.

Through extensive research and development, including collaborations with leading research institutions, we've developed new-generation materials that demonstrate reliable performance at temperatures up to 140°C.

Our Journey

Decades of innovation leading to energy sector excellence

2000s

Foundation & Early Innovation

Established our core polymer science expertise, developing advanced formulation capabilities and building relationships with key research institutions.

2010s

Energy Sector Focus

Pivoted to specialise in energy sector applications, developing our first high-temperature polymer systems for offshore environments.

2015

Advanced Reactor Technology

Invested in state-of-the-art continuous reactor technology, revolutionising our production capabilities and enabling rapid formulation development.

2020

RS3604 Development

Launched our flagship Flexilon RS3604 system, validated for 140°C service temperature applications in the most demanding subsea environments.

2025

Industry Leadership

Recognised as a leading provider of advanced polymer solutions for the global energy sector, with proven performance in major offshore projects worldwide.
