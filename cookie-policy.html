Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

Rosehill Energy uses cookies and similar technologies on our website (energy.rosehill.group) to improve your experience and to understand how our website is being used.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in, or filling in forms.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
rosehill_cookie_consent Stores your cookie consent preferences 1 year

3.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our website. They help us to know which pages are the most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site.

We use Google Analytics via Google Tag Manager to collect this information. All information these cookies collect is aggregated and therefore anonymous.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics - maintains session state 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes users 24 hours
_gat Google Analytics - throttle request rate 1 minute

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking on Cookie Settings.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies you have and delete them on an individual basis
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from particular sites
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Please note that if you choose to block or delete cookies, some features of our website may not work properly.

5. Browser-Specific Instructions

For more information on how to manage cookies in your specific browser, please visit:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

6. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We do not control these third-party cookies and you should check the relevant third party's website for more information about these cookies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last modified" date.
