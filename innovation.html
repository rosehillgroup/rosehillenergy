Pushing the Boundaries of Polymer Chemistry

Innovation & R&D

Our research and development programme focuses on addressing key industry challenges through advanced material science, rigorous testing protocols, and strategic collaboration with leading institutions and operators.

Core Research Areas

Targeted development programmes addressing critical subsea infrastructure challenges

High-Temperature Applications

Developing advanced polymer systems capable of maintaining mechanical properties and chemical resistance at service temperatures up to 140°C in hot-wet conditions.

Marine Environment Performance

Long-term validation testing in natural seawater conditions, including hydrolysis resistance, cathodic disbondment, and accelerated ageing protocols.

Sustainable Polymer Technologies

Pioneering mercury-free formulations and developing next-generation polymer chemistries that meet environmental regulations without compromising performance.

Development Process

Systematic approach to polymer innovation ensuring reliable field performance

01

Material Development

Formulation of novel polymer systems using advanced chemistry, including epoxy-polyurethane hybrids and specialised isocyanate systems for enhanced hot-wet performance.

02

Laboratory Validation

Comprehensive testing including thermal analysis, mechanical property characterisation, and accelerated ageing studies to predict long-term field performance.

03

Field Testing

Real-world validation through collaboration with operators, including subsea exposure testing and performance monitoring under actual service conditions.

04

Process Optimisation

Continuous improvement of processing and application methods to ensure consistent quality, reduced cycle times, and compatibility with existing equipment.

Advanced Reactor Technology

Our state-of-the-art continuous reactor technology represents a step-change in polymer manufacturing capability, enabling rapid formulation of new chemistries while maintaining exceptional quality control.

This technology facilitates accelerated development cycles, allowing us to respond quickly to emerging industry challenges and deliver customised solutions with significantly reduced lead times.

  • Precise temperature and pressure control for optimal reaction conditions
  • Real-time monitoring of reaction parameters
  • Scalable from laboratory to production volumes
  • Reduced batch-to-batch variation
